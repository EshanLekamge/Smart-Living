Wash Room Systems

The best Wash Room System experience in the Smart Home Industry


As the biggest company in the Smart Home Industry, Smart Living provide you with the most Luxurious and the highest quality Wash Room experience with the help of advanced technology.
We offer you the ability to monitor your Wash Room and issue commands from anywhere in the world. This Wash Room system allows you to control your Wash Room and the Devices in your Wash Room at anytime from anywhere as you wish - simply by using your Smartphone or Laptop with internet access.

Our system provides you the ability to control lighting, air-conditioning, heaters, alarms, shutters, doors, windows, window shades, curtains and many more.

We also offer new smart devices such as Smart Showers, Smart Toilets, Smart Bath Tubs, Water Temperature Controllers, Smart Mirrors, Smart Liquid Soap Dispensers, Hand Dryers, Smart Laundry Machines and many other devices.

These Smart devices communicate with each other to operate as a Wash Room system in order to provide you with the most comfortable and luxurious life exeperience.
You can have a Bath in the smart tub, Wash your clothes, Control the water temperature and many more.




With one touch, you can dim or bright the lights, turn up the heater, turn up the air-conditioner, Open or Close the windows, Control the window shades, control the curtains,
lock or unlock the doors, arm or disarm the security system, arm or disarm the alarm system and many things.

You can do all the things only by using your Smartphone such as control the showers and taps, switch on the laundry machine, change the water temperature and many other things.

Our systems makes your life so much easier and comfortable with the help of modern technology.
