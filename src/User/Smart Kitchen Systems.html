Kitchen Systems

The best Smart Kitchen System experience in the Smart Home Industry


As the biggest company in the Smart Home Industry, Smart Living provide you with the most Luxurious and the highest quality Kitchen experience with the help of advanced technology.
We offer you the ability to monitor your Kitchen and issue commands from anywhere in the world. This Kitchen system allows you to control your Kitchen and the Devices in your Kitchen
at anytime from anywhere as you wish - simply by using your Smartphone or Laptop with internet access.

Our system provides you the ability to control lighting, air-conditioning, heaters, alarms, shutters, doors, windows, window shades, curtains and many more.

We also offer new smart devices such as Smart-Refrigarators, Smart Gas Cookers, Smart Rice Cookers, Smart Microwave Ovens, Smart Cofee Machines, Smart Blenders, Smart Kettles and many other devices.

These Smart devices communicate with each other to operate as a Kitchen system in order to provide you with the most comfortable and luxurious life exeperience.You can easily do your cooking and also you can protect you and your family from fires, security breaks and other disasters.




With one touch, you can dim or bright the lights, turn up the heater, turn up the air-conditioner, Open or Close the windows, Control the window shades, control the curtains, lock or unlock the doors, arm or disarm the security system, arm or disarm the alarm system and many things.

You can also make cofee, switch on the blender, boil some water, switch on the microwave oven, control water taps and also you can check the refrigarator to find
which foods are available and which are not. All these things can be easily done using your Smartphone without even visiting your kitchen.

Our systems makes your life so much easier and comfortable with the help of modern technology.
